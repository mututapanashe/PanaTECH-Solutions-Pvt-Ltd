Our Story

Founded in Harare, panaTECH Solutions was born from the observation that businesses in Africa need more than just software; they need systems that actually work in our unique environment.

We are a local team of engineers, designers, and problem-solvers who understand the challenges of connectivity, payments, and adoption in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Whether you're a startup launching an MVP or an enterprise automating workflows, we bring the same level of care, craft, and transparency to every line of code.
